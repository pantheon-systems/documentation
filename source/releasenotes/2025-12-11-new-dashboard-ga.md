---
title: "New Site Dashboard now generally available for all Workspaces"
published_date: "2025-12-11"
categories: [user-interface, new-feature]
---
Starting today, the new Site Dashboard enters General Availability for **all** Workspaces. This includes Professional and Personal Workspaces.

* The new experience is now the default, previously it could be enabled and disabled.
* The option to disable the new experience has been removed.
* The option to opt out of the legacy dashboard experience has been removed from Personal Settings.

Log in today to experience the new Site Dashboard. Share questions and feedback in our Community Slack.
