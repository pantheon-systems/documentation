[Drupal 9.3.22](https://www.drupal.org/project/drupal/releases/9.3.21) is available for production sites and includes backwards-compatible improvements.Â Drupal 9.3 was released in December 2021. From Drupal 9.3, PHP version 8.0 or higher is recommended; PHP 7.3 and higher remains supported. Drupal 9.3.x will receive security coverage until December 8, 2022 when Drupal 9.5.0 is released.

Drupal 9 sites on Pantheon use Integrated Composer, enabling one-click core updates through the Dashboard. To check for available updates, click **Check Now** from the site Dashboard **Dev** tab.
