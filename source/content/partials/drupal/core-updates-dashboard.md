One-click core updates can be made through the Dashboard:

1. Navigate to<Icon icon="code" />**Code** in the <Icon icon="wrench" /> **Dev** tab of your Site Dashboard.

1. Click **Check Now**.

1. Click **Apply Updates** if updates are available.
