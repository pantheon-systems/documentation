---
title: "ionCube has been removed from PHP Runtime Generation 1"
published_date: "2026-03-11"
categories: [infrastructure]
---

[ionCube](https://www.ioncube.com/) support has been removed from PHP Runtime Generation 1. This PHP extension was previously only available for PHP 8.1 and below on Generation 1.

As [previously announced](/release-notes/2025/12/ioncube-removal), this removal is part of our ongoing efforts to optimize performance and build times for the platform.

If your application is impacted by this change, please [contact support](/guides/support/contact-support) or your Customer Success Manager to explore options for your use case.
