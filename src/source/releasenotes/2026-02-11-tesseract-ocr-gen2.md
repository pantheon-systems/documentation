title: Tesseract OCR now available with PHP Runtime Generation 2
published_date: "2026-02-11"
categories: [infrastructure]
---
[Tesseract OCR](https://github.com/tesseract-ocr/tesseract) is now available with [PHP Runtime Generation 2](/php-runtime-generation-2). This open-source optical character recognition (OCR) engine can be used in combination with [Apache Tika](/external-libraries#apache-tika) to enable your sites to extract text from images and scanned documents.
